How It Started

A kitchen drawer, an overcrowded desk, and one question.

It started the way most things do — with frustration. Our founder, Clara, had spent years buying kitchen gadgets that looked great in ads but broke within months, and home office chairs that promised ergonomics but delivered backaches. After one particularly disastrous impulse buy — a "smart" kitchen scale that required three apps and a firmware update just to weigh flour — she decided enough was enough.

NestWell Finds was born from a simple idea: what if someone actually tested this stuff, compared it honestly, and wrote about it without the noise of sponsored placements and inflated star ratings? In 2023, Clara teamed up with Marcus, a former product designer with a passion for minimalist workspaces, and together they began spending weekends testing, photographing, and writing about the tools they genuinely loved.

Today, NestWell Finds reaches readers across Europe and North America who share the same conviction: that a well-chosen tool — whether it's a cast iron skillet or a mechanical keyboard — can fundamentally improve your daily life. We are not a marketplace, we are not sponsored by manufacturers, and we never accept payment to feature a product. Our recommendations are earned, not bought.

We are a participant in the Amazon Associates program, which means we may earn a small commission when you purchase through our links — at no additional cost to you. This is how we keep the lights on, maintain editorial independence, and continue testing products so you don't have to.

What we stand for

Three principles that guide every recommendation we make.

🔬

Research-first

Every product we feature has been thoroughly researched: we read thousands of user reviews, consult expert opinions, and wherever possible, test products hands-on before recommending them.

🤝

Radical transparency

We always disclose when we use affiliate links. We never accept free products in exchange for positive coverage. If something has a flaw, we say so clearly — even if it's otherwise a great product.

⏳

Built to last

We deliberately favour products with long lifespans, quality materials, and reputable brands. We'd rather recommend one excellent skillet than ten mediocre ones. Thoughtful consumption over constant upgrades.
