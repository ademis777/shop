Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept products for review?

We do not accept free products in exchange for reviews. All products featured on NestWell Finds are either purchased independently or thoroughly researched using publicly available information and verified customer reviews.

How do affiliate links work?

When you click a product link and make a purchase, we earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases. This helps us keep the site independent and free of advertising.

Can I suggest a product for you to review?

Absolutely! We love reader suggestions. Use the contact form above and select "Product question" as the subject. If it fits our niches and meets our quality bar, we'll consider featuring it in a future article.
