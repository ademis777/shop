1. Who We Are

NestWell Finds ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website located at https://shop.dmnt.pro (the "Site"). We are an independent editorial platform publishing product reviews and buying guides focused on kitchen gadgets and home office equipment.

For any privacy-related inquiries, you may contact us at: hello@shop.dmnt.pro

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the Site. This includes:

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)

2.2 Data You Provide Voluntarily

  • Name and email address if you submit our contact form
  • Email address if you subscribe to our newsletter

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data. We never require you to create an account to use this Site.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on this Site. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. By continuing to use this Site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this section.

3.1 Essential Cookies

These are strictly necessary for the Site to function. They include the language preference cookie (nw_lang) which stores your chosen display language. These cookies cannot be disabled without breaking the Site.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use Google Analytics (or a privacy-friendly alternative) to understand how visitors use our Site. Analytics cookies collect aggregated, anonymised data. They do not identify you personally. You may opt out of analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3.3 Affiliate & Advertising Cookies (Amazon)

When you click a product link on this Site that directs to an external retailer (including Amazon), that retailer may place cookies on your device to track whether a qualifying purchase is made. This allows us to receive a commission. We have no direct control over these third-party cookies. Please refer to the relevant retailer's privacy policy:

  • Amazon Privacy Notice

3.4 Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this Site and other websites. For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

4. Amazon Associates Program

Affiliate Disclosure: NestWell Finds is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. This means that when you click on a link to an Amazon product and make a purchase, we may receive a commission — at no additional cost to you.

Amazon uses cookies and tracking technologies to attribute purchases to affiliate websites. When you click an affiliate link on our Site, a tracking cookie is set on your device by Amazon. This cookie may persist for up to 24 hours (or until you make a purchase). After a qualifying purchase, the cookie is replaced with a purchase-confirmation cookie.

Our editorial content is never influenced by affiliate relationships. We only recommend products we believe in based on independent research. We never disclose which specific products have higher commission rates, as this has no bearing on our recommendations.

For more information on how Amazon uses data, please see Amazon's Privacy Notice. We do not share your personal data with Amazon; any data collected through Amazon's cookies is governed entirely by Amazon's own privacy policy.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site
  • To respond to contact form submissions and email inquiries
  • To send editorial newsletters (only if you have explicitly subscribed)
  • To analyse Site usage patterns and improve content
  • To earn affiliate commissions that fund our editorial operation
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

The legal basis for processing your personal data (under GDPR) is: legitimate interest (analytics, Site improvement), contract performance (responding to contact form), and consent (newsletter subscription).

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data with the following categories of service providers, who are contractually bound to process it only as instructed and in compliance with applicable data protection law:

  • Analytics providers (e.g. Google Analytics) — anonymised traffic data
  • Email service providers — for sending newsletter emails to confirmed subscribers
  • Hosting providers — for Site infrastructure and security
  • Affiliate networks (Amazon Associates) — via cookie tracking on product links as described above

We may also disclose personal data if required to do so by law, or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation or protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to data portability — You may request a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Right to object — You may object to processing of your data for direct marketing or legitimate interest purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • CCPA (California residents) — You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@shop.dmnt.pro. We will respond within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. Contact form data is retained for up to 12 months. Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained in anonymised, aggregated form. You may request deletion at any time as described above.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Our Site is served over HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Contact & Complaints

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@shop.dmnt.pro
  • Website: shop.dmnt.pro/contact.html
